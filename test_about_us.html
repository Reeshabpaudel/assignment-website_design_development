SMART YATAYAT

SMART WAY TO TRAVEL

Smart Yatayat is a vehicle tracking and management system that aims at minimizing the bridge between public transport and the passengers by introducing smart-transportation system. Visiting new places alone is daunting and often discouraging due to new street names and public transport routes. With Smart Yatayat, travelling is smart and easy with real time vehicle tracking, directions to stations and much more. Travel smart with Smart Yatayat

How it works?

Search any destination

Begin your journey by searching your destination. Once you press enter, you go the next page where you get all the information regarding your travel such as distance to destination, nearest station, arrival time and so on. You can also search with ticket if you already have it.

Track your bus

In bus travel, not knowing when the next bus is causes anxiety even hating to have to travel. Not anymore! By using ‘Track My Bus’ feature you can see the exact location of the bus in line map in real-time. Your travel is now hassle-free!

Book ticket

Book a ticket and reserve your seat once you are clear with directions and timings. What’s more is you can choose the timing that fits you the best. Book early and book your lucky seat the next time you travel!

Directions to sation

Okay, so you know where to go, which bus to take and when it’ll arrive. But where is the damn station? Worry not when you can get directions to the nearest station in just a click. Interact with the map and you are good to enjoy your bus travel.
